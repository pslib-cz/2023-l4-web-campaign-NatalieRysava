    The Royal Mint

    Crystal coin

    The unique “Crystal Coin” gift series is the result of the combination of traditional Jablonec craftsmanship-glassmaking and the minting process. As such, we have combined precious metals with Bohemian crystal as part of the Czech Mint’s limited edition plans. The most popular motifs include the birth of a child, a guardian angel, the Tree of Life or signs of the zodiac.
