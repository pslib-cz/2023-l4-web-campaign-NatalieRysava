    European Enterprise Promotion Awards

    Grand Jury prize

    The trophy for the winner of the European Enterprise Promotion Awards, which recognises and rewards the most successful public sector projects focussing on support for entrepreneurship and enterprise throughout Europe.

    Preciosa excellently worked with the provided specifications and used its glassmaking skill to combine the historic panorama of Prague with the iconic symbol of the metro.

    The glassmakers selected a combination of several technologies that gave rise to the dimples that are typical for the tiles in Prague’s metro stations, while the hand polished crystal is literally embraced by Prague’s architecture in the form of the gilt metal strip.
