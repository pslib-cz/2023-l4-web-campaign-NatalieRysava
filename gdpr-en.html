    Privacy Statement

    PRECIOSA, a.s., PRECIOSA – LUSTRY, a.s., PRECIOSA ORNELA, a.s., PRECIOSA BEAUTY, s.r.o., VINOLOK, a.s., and their subsidiaries (collectively "Preciosa") strive to protect your personal data and ensure your satisfaction with the use of our websites, products and services.

    This privacy statement applies to Preciosa websites and online stores that link to this statement, describe how personal data is processed, and explain your ability to collect, use, update and correct personal information and access it.

    Some Preciosa websites may contain a separate statement of personal privacy, which describes how personal data is processed on that particular website. In the event that, at the time of data collection, a note or statement appears on a specific website regarding the protection of personal data that is inconsistent with this privacy statement, the note or statement on the specific website prevails.

    Collection of Personal Data

    Information, including personal data, may be collected when you use a Preciosa website, online e-shop, or when you communicate with Preciosa. "Personal Data" is defined as any information that can be used to identify a person and may include names, addresses, e-mail addresses, telephone numbers, login information (account number, password), marketing preferences, social media account information, or credit card numbers. If other data is linked to your personal data, Preciosa treats all such collected data as personal. Preciosa also collects personal data from trusted third-party sources and engages third parties that assist in the collection of personal information.

    Preciosa collects personal data for a number of reasons, including:

    • Order processing, including payment transactions.
    • Providing new product information.
    • Sending marketing updates.
    • Creating an account.
    • Improving service delivery/your experience.
    • Providing customer service.
    • Processing job applications.

    Preciosa and the third parties with whom it cooperates may aggregate information gathered over time on Preciosa websites or online stores with information obtained from other sources. This helps improve overall data accuracy and completeness and thus better tailor Preciosa’s communication with you.

    If you choose to provide Preciosa with personal data of a third party (such as names, e-mail addresses, or phone numbers), you agree and declare that you have the third party’s permission to do so. Examples include the forwarding of marketing materials to a friend or the referral of a potential employee. Third parties may withdraw from any future communication via the link provided in each marketing message or by sending an e-mail to privacy@preciosa.com.

    In some cases, Preciosa and the third parties with whom it cooperates automatically collect data using cookies, web protocols, web signals, and other similar applications. This information is used to better understand and improve the usability, performance, and efficiency of websites and to help tailor content or offers to users. For more information, see “Use of Cookies and Other Technologies” below.

    Use of Personal Data

    Preciosa may use personal data for general business operations as well as to deliver, improve and customize its websites and online shops, send marketing and other business communication, and for other legitimate purposes applicable by law.

    Some of the ways Preciosa may use personal data include:

    • Delivery of ordered products.
    • Analysis, support, and improvement of Preciosa solutions and your online experience.
    • Customization of websites, new products, and general communications.
    • Communication, including marketing information and measuring customer satisfaction, either directly from Preciosa or one of its partners.

    Each individual can edit his/her communications preferences at any time. See "Personal and Communication Preferences" below.

    Access to Personal Data, Accuracy and Individual Rights

    Preciosa processes personal data in accordance with the law, ethically, and with full transparency. When processing data, we respect an individual’s rights to:

    • Access personal data and other related information.
    • Correct inaccurate information or to clarify the data we process.
    • Delete data (the right to be forgotten) if the data processing is unjustified or if the reasons for the data processing are obsolete.
    • Limit processing.
    • Data portability in a structured or machine-readable form, which corresponds to the method of storing such data.
    • Object to the processing of such data.
    • Not be subject to automatic decision-making or profiling.
    • Withdraw consent to the processing of your personal data at any time, unless it is inconsistent with legitimate interests or legal obligations.
    • File a complaint with the Office for Personal Data Protection at: Pplk. Sochora 27, 170 00 Praha 7.

    In order to manage the accuracy and timeliness of personal information, Preciosa needs your help. We provide a number of options for accessing, correcting, restricting or removing your personal information:

    • Personal data contained in a profile can be viewed and edited on any Preciosa website and online store that includes registration.
    • Some Preciosa companies can be considered to be data managers and/or data processors, or they can operate as such. If a Preciosa entity acts as a data manager or data processor, access rights can be used to request changes, add limitations, or deactivate data under the applicable data protection laws directly with the specific Preciosa entity.
    • If you need further assistance with access, data correction, or the limiting or deleting of personal data, please do not hesitate to contact Preciosa directly. Preciosa tries, in good faith, to honour reasonable requests for access, removal, updating, limiting, or change of data. Preciosa will respond to any request within 30 days. If unable to comply with your request, Preciosa will provide you with an explanation.

    Use of Cookies and Other Web Technologies

    Preciosa websites and online stores use automatic data collection tools, such as cookies, embedded web links, and web feeds. These tools collect some of the standard information that your browser sends to the Preciosa website. Examples of such information include the type of browser or website address from which you reached the Preciosa website.

    These tools may also collect information regarding the following items:

    • Your Internet Protocol (IP) address. This is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer or device whenever you connect to the Internet. This is a unique address assigned by your ISP or IT department on the TCP/IP network. Additionally, the IP address allows web servers to find and identify your devices;
    • Behaviour on visited web pages. Examples include pages you have viewed and the links you have clicked on. These tools help make it easier to streamline and improve your visits to Preciosa websites by providing customized experiences and recognizing when you return.

    Preciosa web pages contain widgets. Widgets are miniature interactive programs run on our site to provide certain services from other companies. Examples of such services include viewing messages, opinions, videos, and other content. Using these widgets, personal data, such as an e-mail address, may be collected. Widgets can also set cookies to ensure they work properly. Information gathered by widgets is governed by the privacy policy of the company which created the widget.

    We work with third parties to manage our ads on other sites. Our partners can use cookies or similar technologies to show ads based on your activities while browsing the Internet and your interests. You have the right to express your explicit disagreement with the showing of such ads.

    By disagreeing and opting out of such ads you will continue to see general ads that are not tailored to your interests. To disagree with tailored or custom ads, please click:

    • Here, if you are in the European Union;
    • Here, if you are elsewhere in the world.
