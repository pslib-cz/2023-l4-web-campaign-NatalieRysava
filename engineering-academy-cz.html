    Engineering Academy of the Czech Republic

    Trophy for the winning project

    Propojení tradiční sklářské výroby The combination of traditional glassmaking production with science and technology. This encapsulates the cooperation between Preciosa and the Engineering Academy of the Czech Republic, which recognises excellent technical works and significant contributions to the development of engineering in the Czech Republic each year.

    The award has been designed as a stylisation of Ressel’s ship’s propeller, which the Engineering Academy has included in its logo. The shape slightly evokes the curve of a propeller and at the same time is reminiscent of the silhouette of a cup, i.e. a traditional trophy. The award is in an elegant shade of blue and is completely made of Czech crystal.
